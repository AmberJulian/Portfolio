Let me introduce myself

Personality:

I'm a developer living in Melbourne, Australia. I love video games, Netflix, cats, and baking. Lately, I've gotten into bike riding - there are some amazing paths around Melbourne!

I'm originally from a small farmer town in New Zealand called Bombay. One of the best parts of growing up there Next door growing up was a paddock of soft friendly cows - they inspired a love of nature and being outdoors.

Being in a small country town meant there wasn't much to do during the day at times. That's how I got into video gaming.

My passion for video games eventually lead me to move to Auckland to study software engineering at Media Design School. Originally, I tried it because I felt more confident in my math abilities than in art. But I grew to appreciate that programming itself is an artform - one that I love and am good at!

Following my successes in Media Design School, I quickly landed a job in game development. Now, having been there for 6 years, I've worked on many cool projects, and even worked my way up to management.

Now I'm seeking something to to learn. I have my sights set on professional web development.
